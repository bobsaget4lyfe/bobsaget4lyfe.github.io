Biography

Justin Dixon is a recent graduate from the University of Sydney. He studied his Master of Economic Analysis and focused his studies on micro economic theory and applied micro economics. For his research thesis he researched various applications of contest theory with novel data from professional Dota 2 matches.

After graduating with his Bachelors Justin spent a year travelling and working in Europe. After returning to Australia he worked briefly as a tax accountant before training to become an economist.
